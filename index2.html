BD Workflows

Specialized workflows for deal sourcing, target evaluation, and due diligence acceleration.

Deal Signal Detection

Monitors SEC filings and earnings transcripts for partnership intent, "strategic alternatives" language, and M&A signals across your target universe.

Surfaces deal-relevant language from 10-Ks, 8-Ks, and management commentary.

Competitive Landscape

Generates competitive positioning analysis with efficacy benchmarks, differentiation factors, and market dynamics for target therapeutic areas.

Structures competitive intelligence across clinical, commercial, and strategic dimensions.

Global Pipeline Intelligence

Tracks pipeline developments across NMPA filings, Chinese biotech sources, and regulatory portals beyond standard database coverage.

Extends visibility into China and emerging markets with native-language monitoring.

Trial Forecasting

Monitors enrollment status, protocol amendments, and regulatory interactions to anticipate timeline shifts and upcoming catalysts.

Tracks ClinicalTrials.gov updates and enrollment signals for catalyst timing.

Deal Valuation

Analyzes comparable transactions and generates valuation ranges based on development stage, indication, and deal structure precedents.

Structures deal comps by stage, modality, and therapeutic area for valuation benchmarking.

Rights & Territory Analysis

Maps licensing rights and identifies assets with territory availability from partnership disclosures, SEC filings, and press releases.

Identifies ex-China, ex-US, and regional licensing opportunities across target assets.
